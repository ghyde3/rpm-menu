**Tag** — tiny caps chip for item attributes on the menu and CMS list.

```jsx
<Tag tone="new">New</Tag>
<Tag tone="spicy">Master Blaster</Tag>
<Tag tone="fave">Fan Fave</Tag>
<Tag>GF Bun</Tag>
```

Tones: `new` (green fill), `spicy` (red fill), `veggie` (green outline), `fave` (amber outline), `default` (steel outline). Keep labels 1–2 short words.
